Privacy Policy for Life Nexus Application

Date of Publication: November 2025

1. Testnet Phase Status

This application (Life Nexus) is currently operating in the **Testnet** phase and is intended for development and testing purposes only. Any data processed is for experimental purposes and does not involve real financial transactions.

2. Collection of Personal Data

The Life Nexus application does not currently collect or store any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) from users.

We only use the Pi SDK's Authentication function to verify the identity of Pi Network users (username) and will not use any other information.

3. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the application transitions to the Mainnet. Users will be notified of any substantial changes before they take effect.
